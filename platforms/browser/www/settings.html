I'm Hidden From

By default, everyone you meet will not be able to see Invites that you post. You may change that below.

Additionally, you may block anybody from seeing Invites that you post by simply entering their cell number. This applies to both current members and future members.

So if you're worried about your coworker joining and seeing your invites, enter his/her cell number.

  • Cell Numbers who cannot see my Invites (1)
